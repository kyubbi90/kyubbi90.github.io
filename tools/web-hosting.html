Web Hosting

The free web hosting option that I found is from Weebly. Weebly’s free option is vastly limited compared to it’s paid options. The storage limit on the free option is 500MB, while the paid options are unlimited. There is no option to remove any Weebly branding and you can’t connect a previously purchased domain. I did previously use Weebly for a class in middle school and had no problems using it when I was at that academic level. The paid site I found is HostGator, which has the better services compared to Weebly. Even the lowest priced deal has unmetered bandwidth and storage. The site also offers cloud hosting for an extra two dollars a month. So based on comparisons between Weebly and HostGator, HostGator has the better service for someone who wants a cheap and customizable website.
