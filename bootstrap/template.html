Bootstrap Template

I did a simple google search for "free bootstrap templates" and it took me a bit of browsing through the sites to find something that I really liked. A lot of bootstrap templates that are out there are for shops. This does make sense as people who want to sell a product may not want to put in the effort into learning how to make websites and would want to easily set up a site. One template I found somewhat interesting looking was Clean Blog. After looking a bit more I found another template that I liked the look of much more called Slate from Bootswatch. Bootswatch has a good amount of templates that are very similar with different palettes and fonts.
