Understanding SEO

What is SEO for e-commerce?

  • SEO - Search Engine Optimization
  • Traffic must be increased to help ranking
  • Assessment of factors to improve search engine rankings.
  • Only Google knows how the ranking algorithm works

Understanding on-page vs off-page SEO

  • On-page factors - keywords, written content, page urls and internal links
  • Off-page factors - social media , blog posts , and product reviews.

The Importance of usability

  • Usability is based on keeping consumers on the site and to have them return
  • Design for customers before search engines
  • Donâ€™t bury information present it to the user.

Exploring how SEO affects the buying process

  • SEO can be used throughout the buying process

Setting expectations and establishing realistic goals for your SEO results

  • Results may shift up and down
  • Results are not immediate, as it's an ongoing process

Researching Your Customers

Understanding exactly how your customer is

  • Surveys and interviews can be used to find customer demographics
  • Market towards those demographics to get customers

Creating buyer personas

  • Buyer personas - determine age, gender, location, job, and interests
  • Apply those to a real person and design the site so it can easily be used by that person
  • Quality traffic = higher sales.

Using simple and long tail keyword searches

  • Simple keywords - 1-2 words targeting broad service, difficult to target
  • Long-tail keywords - specific, 3+ words, and easier to target

Getting the most out of keyword research

  • Write initial list of keywords and use Google suggestions to find similar searches
  • Search keywords through Google keyword planner
  • Quality over quantity for keyword list

Carrying out competitor research

  • Research competitors identify other sites for that keywords
  • SEMrush shows main competitors of sites and their big keywords.

Auditing for Site Usability and Structure

Harnessing page titles and meta information

  • Page title should 50-60 characters, use keywords at beginning, and using unique titles
  • Page descriptions should be clear and 150-160 characters long

Exploring page structure: Headings, copy, and images

  • Page heading is the first thing seen on a site.
  • Page heading draws the viewer's attention
  • Use alt tag to describe images to boost ranking

Crafting internal links to help search engines explore your site

  • Anchor text can be used as keywords as they link to other sites
  • No generic anchor tags
  • Large footer for navigation is helpful for user

Earning inbound links to raise your ranking

  • External link - any link that links to your site
  • Product review sites can be used to boost your sites ratings
  • Use quality inbound links over many inbound links
